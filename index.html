Welcome to my Mushroom Sweeper game. It's basically minesweeper. The goal of the game is to find all the Teemo Mushrooms by right clicking on the squares where you think they are and left clicking on all the areas that contain no mushrooms. At first, whether or not there is a mushroom in the square is hidden. After left clicking a number will apear in the square if there is a mushroom nearby (one block away from the square). If you left click on a square with a mushroom hidden inside it, you die. Good luck and have fun!

Also, you can change the board size and difficulty. Just navigate to the bottom of the page.
